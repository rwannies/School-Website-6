About Our School

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and compassion in a supportive environment.

We are committed to nurturing each student's unique talents, encouraging a love for learning, and preparing them for global citizenship.

Our dedicated staff works tirelessly to create an environment where every child feels valued and empowered.

Our History

Founded in 1980, Despatch Preparatory School has been a cornerstone of quality education in our community for over four decades.

Our legacy includes thousands of graduates who have gone on to excel in various fields, carrying forward our values of integrity and excellence.

We continue to build on this foundation, adapting to modern educational needs while staying true to our core principles.

Core Curriculum

Comprehensive programs in Math, Science, Language Arts, and Social Studies.
